Our Story

Honesty, Quality & Relationships.

Since our establishment in 1993, Shalibhadra Traders has been driven by the belief in honesty, quality, and long-term relationships. Guided by strong ethical business practices, we have earned trust as one of the most reliable names in the Indian spice industry.

For us, quality is a commitment. We consistently meet and exceed customer expectations by delivering spices that embody freshness, authenticity, and value—always on time.

Infrastructure & Quality

"Quality with Quantity"

Living up to our motto, we maintain excellence across sourcing, processing, packaging, and supply. We procure directly from farmers and process in our 30 TPD automatic Sortex & Cleaning plant, ensuring purity, consistency, and superior quality.

  • 30 TPD Automatic Sortex & Cleaning Plants
  • Direct procurement from farmers for authentic purity
  • Strict quality control for uniformity & freshness
